Project Journal & Findings

Course: Visual Analytics  ·  Water Consumption Tableau

Audience & Intent

The audience for this dashboard activists for pollution and renewable resources, scientists and students, politicians/government officials and engineers. The dashboard should show trends on water availability and consumption, and it can help determine time periods or events that may have correlated to the availability or consumption. The dashboard also allows users to see trends of both scarcity and consumption, and how its being used. This can help activists identify points and years of interest, and help engineers or government officials identify when these incidents occur and how to maintain water availability.

KPIs

Firstly, the target KPIs would be low water scarcity (to indicate availability), and the target for water consumption should be relative, as in high consumption but low scarcity is a target, but high consumption high scarcity is the worst.

  • Water Scarcity: I chose water scarcity because this is extremely crucial as it is relative to availability and consumption. It doesn't matter if you consume less water, if water is still scarce, evem if they can correlate.
  • Water Consumption: This KPI can show many things. It can suggest very high activity (industrial, AI/tech, agriculture). Not only that, but it shows original availability, and it is also needed to see the proportion for scarcity.
  • Consumption Type %: This is similar to water consumption, but can help see where water usage is going. I predict that if the AI bubble does not pop, industrial water will increase in many countries with data centers.

Design Choices/Dashboard Structure

I used a line chart to show trends of water consumption on a large scale. However, I also have a bar chart, that you can control and show each year's water consumption and scarcity, so it is interactive and can help reach target KPIs. Furthermore I chose a pie chart since for the types of water consumption, since its only 3 features and gives essential info.

Data cleaning

No data cleaning was needed, however I did have to filter the countries for the line chart or it would get too cluttered, so I chose the highest 10 in water consumption.

Reflection
